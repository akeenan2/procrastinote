Notes about tigers Color  Tigers are orange
  Tigers have stripes
 Habitat  Tigers live in the jungle
 Behavior  Tigers hunt for food
 Random  Tigers are the largest member of the cat family
  Tigers can take down a 500 lb antelope
  Tigers can swim
  There are nine subspecies: Siberian, Bengal, Indochinese, Sumatran, South Chinese, Malayan, Javan, Caspian and Bali
  Despite being ferocious, tigers are quite similar to household kittens
 o It costs about $2,500 for a 2 month old tiger, but there is one seller online who will trade you it for black bear or cougar cub
